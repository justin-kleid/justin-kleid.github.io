I have spent hours defining x, 
{: .text-justify}
As the numbers become complex. 
{: .text-justify}
But poor x feels drowned, 
{: .text-justify}
He hates to be found.  
{: .text-justify}
Strict definitions shroud his apex. 

{: .text-justify}
{: .text-justify}

Justin Kleidermacher
